💡 Tips
➕
Adding a Habit
Tap the + button on the bottom left. Give your habit a name, pick a category (Health, Mind, Sleep, Nutrition) and choose its XP difficulty — Easy (10), Medium (20) or Hard (30).
✅
Completing a Habit
Tap the circle button on the left of any habit to check it off for today. You'll earn XP instantly. You can also tap individual day dots to mark past days.
🔥
Streaks
Complete a habit on consecutive days to build a streak. The flame counter under each habit tracks how many days in a row you've kept it up. Don't break the chain!
🏷️
Categories & Filters
Use the filter tabs (All / 💪 Health / 🧘 Mind / 🌙 Sleep / 🍎 Nutrition) to focus on one area. Each category has its own colour tag on the habit card.
📝
Notes
Tap + note on any habit card to add a personal reminder or motivation tip. It shows as a small preview below the habit name.
💡 Pro tip: Start with 3–5 habits maximum. It's better to crush a few every day than to overwhelm yourself and quit.
⚡
Earning XP
Every habit you complete gives XP based on its difficulty — 10 XP (Easy), 20 XP (Medium), 30 XP (Hard). Completing daily quests and unlocking achievements also awards bonus XP.
🏆
10 Warrior Ranks
As you earn XP you rise through ranks — from Iron Squire all the way to Immortal God. Each rank has its own custom dark fantasy emblem that appears on your XP banner and profile.
📊
XP Bar
The gold bar at the top of the Habits page shows your progress to the next rank. The number on the right shows your current XP vs what's needed to level up.
🌟
Level Up
When you reach a new rank a Level Up screen appears with your new emblem and title. Tap "Claim your power" to continue.
💡 Monthly XP resets each month so you can track how consistent you are month over month. Your total rank XP never resets.
📜
What Are Daily Quests?
Every day at midnight, 4 fresh quests are generated for you. They're based on your habits and give bonus XP on top of your normal habit rewards.
⚔️
Quest Types
Quests include things like "Complete 3 habits today", "Finish all your health habits", "Keep a streak alive" or "Log a workout". Progress updates automatically as you use the app.
🎉
Quest Complete Popup
When you finish a quest a full-screen celebration pops up with particle effects and your XP reward. Tap "Claim Reward" to close it.
🔄
Refreshing Quests
Tap the 🔄 refresh icon in the top right of the Quests page to generate a new set of quests at any time. Useful if today's quests don't suit your plan.
💡 Badge on nav: The number on the Quests tab shows how many quests are still uncompleted today — keep it at zero!
🏋️
Logging a Workout
Tap the + button on the Gym page (or the FAB when on Gym tab). Give the session a name (e.g. "Push Day"), then add exercises with sets, reps and weight in kg.
📈
Progress Chart
The chart at the top of the Gym page shows your total volume (kg × sets × reps) over time. Use the dropdown to filter by a specific exercise to track your strength progress on that movement.
💪
Gym Stats
The three stats at the top show total workouts logged, how many this week, and total kg lifted across all sessions ever.
💡 Logging a workout automatically counts toward the "Iron Trial" daily quest and the "Gym Rat" achievement — +25 XP every session!
📱
Install on Your Phone
iPhone: Open in Safari → tap Share → "Add to Home Screen". Android: Open in Chrome → tap ⋮ menu → "Add to Home Screen". This gives you a real app icon and full-screen experience.
🔔
Reminders
Tap the 🔔 bell icon in the top right to set morning and evening reminder times. Reminders show as alerts when you open the app at those times.
🎨
Themes
Go to Profile → scroll down to Themes. Choose between Dark Fantasy (default), Blood Red or Ocean Blue.
⚠️
Streak Warning
If you have a streak of 3+ days on a habit and haven't checked it in today, a streak warning will appear at the top of the screen reminding you before it resets.
💾
Your Data
All data is saved locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. If you clear your browser data or uninstall, your progress will be lost — so install it to your Home Screen to keep it safe.
💡 14 achievements to unlock — check the Quests tab to see which ones you're close to. Each gives bonus XP!
⚡ Habit Templates
One-tap habit stacks used by warriors. Tap any stack to add all habits instantly.
